Founding & Mission

Founding

The University of Toledo Maker Society is a Student Organization that was founded September 18, 2014. The organization is open to students from any field of study, and currently has around 20 active members

The goal behind the birth of the organization was to start a makerspace for students to have the resources to create their own projects, and build a community of diversely skilled, creative individuals.
